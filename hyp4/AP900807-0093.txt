An Iraqi airways jet carrying 78 foreigners and a number of Iraqi citizens arrived today the first commercial flight allowed out of Iraq since it invaded Kuwait a Japanese Embassy spokesman said
The mostly Japanese tourists arrived at Amman airport at 615 pm (1115 am EDT) along with 34 members of an Iraqi football team said the spokesman on condition of anonymity
He said that the plane was due to leave Amman for an undisclosed destination
Jordan's information minister Ibrahim Izzedine said earlier today that the US government had asked Jordan to facilitate the entrance from Iraq of American diplomats and their dependents
