In a major rebuff to the Soviet Union's scientific establishment Soviet physicists Friday nominated Nobel laureate Andrei D Sakharov to run as a candidate in nationwide parliamentary elections scheduled for March
Although Sakharov has publicly expressed his support for President Mikhail S Gorbachev in recent months he seems to have become increasingly concerned about the slow and uneven pace of reform
Sakharov who acted as an unofficial Kremlin envoy to the troubled Transcaucasian region last month said he was particularly dismayed by a government crackdown against leading Armenian nationalists
