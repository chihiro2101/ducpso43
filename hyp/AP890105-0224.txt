Retired Sen John Tower the defense secretary-designate underwent surgery Thursday to remove a colon polyp a hospital spokesman said
``Senator Tower is resting comfortably and is listed in fair condition'' hospital spokesman Steve Habgood said Thursday night
Tower 63 a US senator from Texas for 24 years was admitted to Baylor University Medical Center on Wednesday after undergoing a colonscopy examination last week that revealed the polyp Habgood said
He also served as an arms negotiator for President Reagan and he headed the White House's Iran-Contra investigative panel
