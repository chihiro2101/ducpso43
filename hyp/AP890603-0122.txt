Here is a chronology of events that began Saturday in Tiananmen Square
1 am Saturday (12 noon EDT Friday)
_ Tens of thousands of troops enter Beijing from the east marching toward Tiananmen Square China's symbolic political heart
Troops straggle away from their positions near the square walking in small groups followed by watchful residents
Two truckloads of People's Armed Police attack crowds outside Beijing Hotel east of the square and up to 30 people injured
2 pm _ About 5000 troops emerge from Great Hall of the People on the western side of the square and are surrounded by crowds
Thousands of residents surround two troop convoys on a main highway in northeastern Beijing
