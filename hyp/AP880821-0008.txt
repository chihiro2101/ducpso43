A strong earthquake hit the Himalayan kingdom of Nepal and parts of eastern India on Sunday morning triggering landslides and collapsing buildings
First reports said 100 people may have died
The department said the quake was centered near Udayapur a town near the Indian-Nepalese border and 100 miles southeast of Katmandu Nepal's capital
In New Delhi the Seismologoical Observatory said the quake measured 65 on the Richter scale
Press Trust of India news agency said that in addition to 100 feared dead in India and Nepal 1000 people were injured in the Indian states of Bihar Sikkim and West Bengal
