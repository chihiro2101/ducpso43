Hundreds of helmeted troops were deployed on the monument to revolutionary heroes in the square's center and thousands more at important intersections nearby
``I'm not afraid'' several said as the heavily armed troops encircled
Loudspeakers blaring government and student announcements engaged in a noisy propaganda battle throughout the night
The state-run speaker warned that troops would use every means at their disposal to clear the square and that remaining students would bear responsibility for the consequences
After the lights went back on a half hour later a voice vote by the students showed most wanted to leave the square to avoid further bloodshed
