A yacht built for Adolf Hitler and sunk with great fanfare to commemorate the ``Voyage of the Damned'' was mistakenly dropped on a delicate coral reef in a shallow shipping lane officials said Tuesday
``They're going to have to move it'' said Coast Guard spokeswoman Veronica Cady
It was sunk June 4 50 years after the US Coast Guard turned away the SS St Louis as its more than 900 Jewish passengers were seeking refuge from the Holocaust
